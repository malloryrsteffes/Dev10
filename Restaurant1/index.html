About Us

Mom's Magic Munchems started out at a kitchen stove and is now one of the most-loved restaurants of the St. Pete area. Now over 10 years old, MMMs continues to delight St. Pete residents and visitors with its original dishes and addicting appetizers.

Mom's Magic Munchems was begun in the kitchen of a loving mom, who wanted her family to eat right AND enjoy themselves during meals. After countless compliments and suggestions from friends and strangers to share her delicious recipes, she realized she'd found a new calling. Mom's Magic Munchems was opened to spread the joy of healthy-ish, delicious eating to anyone who cared to drop in.
